Handong Christian Event Hub

The Handong Christian Event Hub is your go-to platform for staying connected with spiritual life at Handong Global University. This hub brings together worship services, Bible study sessions, prayer gatherings, and special events to foster faith and fellowship. Whether you're looking to grow spiritually, join a small group, or participate in community outreach, the Handong Christian Event Hub is here to guide and inspire you. Together, let's celebrate faith, build meaningful relationships, and serve with purpose!
